## Government Notifications

### THE EXCISE ORDINANCE (CHAPTER 52)
**Excise Notification No. 01/2022**

BY virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 22(1) of the Excise Ordinance (Chapter 52), as amended from time to time, I, Basil Rajapaksa, Minister of Finance, do hereby order that every person who manufactures or imports any excisable article, shall pay an excise duty on such excisable article at the rates set out in the Schedule hereto, with effect from January 15, 2022.

The Excise Notification No. 04/2021 published in the Extraordinary Gazette No. 2247/14 dated September 29, 2021 is hereby rescinded.
